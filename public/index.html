How to Play

Welcome to the Wordle Clone! Here's how to play:

  • Guess the secret five-letter word within six attempts.
  • After each guess, the color of the tiles will change to show how close your guess was to the word.
  • Green: The letter is in the correct spot.
  • Yellow: The letter is in the word but in the wrong spot.
  • Gray: The letter is not in the word.
  • Click "Reset Game" to start a new game.
  • You get 60 points if you guess the word in the first guess, and for each further guess, the total possible points reduce by 10.
  • View the "Leaderboard" to see the top scores.

Good luck!
